# Bubble Pop

## Template
TapGame

## Concept
Colorful bubbles float up from the bottom of the screen. Kid taps them to pop.

## Toy Shelf Icon
Bubble wand -- blue circle with shine highlight

## Mechanics
- Bubbles spawn at bottom, float upward with gentle wobble
- Tap a bubble to pop it
- New bubble spawns on each pop
- Bubbles that float off the top disappear (no penalty)
- Auto-spawn on timer (~0.8s) to keep screen populated

## Visual
- 5 bubble colors: sky blue, pink, yellow, blue, purple
- 45px radius (big tap target)
- White shine highlight on each bubble (top-left)
- Semi-transparent fill (0.8 alpha)

## Feedback
- Pop: bubble disappears instantly + confetti burst
- Score displayed at top center

## Difficulty
- None. No pressure, no timer, no fail state.
- Bubbles move slowly (40-100px/s)

## Sound (planned)
- Tap: pop sound
- Background: soft underwater ambience
