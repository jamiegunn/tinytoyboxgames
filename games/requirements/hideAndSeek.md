# Hide and Seek Animals

## Template
TapGame

## Concept
Animals peek out from behind objects (trees, rocks, bushes). Kid taps them when they appear.

## Toy Shelf Icon
Tree with cartoon eyes peeking from behind

## Mechanics
- Scene: outdoor setting with hiding spots (trees, rocks, bushes, flowers)
- Animals peek out from behind hiding spots at random intervals
- Peek cycle: appear (slide out), hold for 2-3 seconds, disappear (slide back)
- Tap while visible to "find" the animal
- Found animals do a happy animation, then a new animal starts peeking elsewhere
- Multiple animals can peek at once (increase over time)

## Visual
- Background: green grass, blue sky, simple landscape
- Hiding spots: large simple shapes (green triangles for trees, grey ovals for rocks)
- Animals: simple cartoon faces -- bunny ears, cat whiskers, bear snout
- Peek animation: animal slides sideways from behind the object
- Found animation: animal jumps up with happy expression

## Feedback
- Find: animal bounces + confetti
- Streak of fast finds: big celebration
- Counter: animals found

## Difficulty
- Peek duration is generous (2-3 seconds)
- Animals are large when peeking
- Start with 1 peeker, slowly increase to 2-3
- No penalty for missing a peek

## Sound (planned)
- Peek: rustling leaves
- Found: happy animal sound (varies by animal)
- Background: outdoor birds/wind
