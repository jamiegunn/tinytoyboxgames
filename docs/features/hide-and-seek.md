# Hide And Seek

## What it is

Hide And Seek is a peek-and-find game where animals briefly appear from behind trees, bushes, and rocks.

## How it plays

1. The scene lays out several hiding spots across a grassy landscape.
2. Animals peek out from one side of a spot, pause, then retreat.
3. The player taps the visible animal before it disappears.
4. Found animals pop into a short floating reveal animation.

## Controls

- Tap a peeking animal.

## Scoring and progression

- Each found animal adds 1 point.
- The game starts with only one active peeker at a time.
- At 5 points it can run two simultaneous peekers.
- At 12 points it can run three simultaneous peekers.
- Missing a peeker has no punishment; another one eventually appears.

## Feedback and presentation

- The scene uses a bright sky, drifting clouds, hills, and simple hiding-spot art.
- Successful finds play a chirpy sound and trigger confetti.
- The active animals are bunny, cat, bear, and frog.
