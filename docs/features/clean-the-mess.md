# Clean The Mess

## What it is

Clean The Mess is a reveal game where the player scrubs away a muddy overlay to uncover hidden pictures underneath.

## How it plays

1. A full-screen mud layer hides a set of illustrated objects.
2. The player drags or taps to erase the mud with a large circular scrub brush.
3. Individual pictures become "found" once enough mud is cleared around them.
4. After every hidden picture is revealed, the round ends and the player can tap to start another one.

## Controls

- Drag to scrub continuously.
- Tap to make a single scrub mark.
- After the round is complete, tap anywhere to move to the next round.

## Scoring and progression

- The top counter tracks how many full cleanup rounds have been completed.
- It does not score each hidden item individually.
- Each round contains at least 5 hidden pictures, and later rounds can include more.
- A per-round progress bar shows how many of that round's objects have been found.

## Hidden picture set

The reveal pool includes sun, star, fish, flower, heart, moon, cloud, tree, butterfly, rainbow, mushroom, and snail illustrations.

## Feedback and presentation

- Early partial cleaning can trigger an "Almost!" pop-up before the final reveal.
- Fully revealing an item plays a squeaky wipe sound, triggers confetti, and clears the surrounding mud.
- Clearing the entire board triggers a bigger particle burst and an "All Clean! Tap for more!" message.
