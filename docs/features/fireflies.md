# Fireflies

## What it is

Fireflies is a nighttime tap game about catching glowing bugs and watching them collect inside a jar.

## How it plays

1. Fireflies drift around the sky in gentle curved paths.
2. The player taps near a firefly to catch it.
3. Caught fireflies arc down into the jar at the bottom of the screen.
4. New fireflies keep spawning so the game continues endlessly.

## Controls

- Tap near a firefly to catch it.

## Scoring and progression

- Standard fireflies are worth 1 point before combo bonuses.
- Rare golden fireflies are worth 5 points before combo bonuses.
- Catching multiple fireflies within 3.5 seconds builds a combo.
- The number of active fireflies increases with score, up to a capped maximum.
- The jar visually fills with up to 25 captured lights.

## Feedback and presentation

- The scene includes stars, clouds, a moon, silhouette trees, rolling hills, and glowing grass.
- Fireflies leave light trails and pulse with different glow colors.
- Each catch triggers a bell-like sound and confetti.
- Combo streaks display as a "CATCH xN" meter.
