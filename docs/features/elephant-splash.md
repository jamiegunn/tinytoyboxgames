# Elephant Splash

## What it is

Elephant Splash is a water-spraying target game. The player aims an elephant's trunk and soaks animated targets until they are fully wet.

## How it plays

1. Tapping or dragging aims the elephant's trunk.
2. A short tap triggers a brief burst; dragging keeps the spray running.
3. Water particles travel across the scene and gradually fill each target's wetness meter.
4. Once a target is soaked enough, it pops out in a splash animation and a new target eventually respawns.

## Controls

- Tap to aim and spray briefly.
- Drag to keep spraying continuously.
- Releasing the drag stops the spray.

## Scoring and progression

- Standard soaked targets score combo-scaled points.
- Hitting targets within 2.5 seconds of each other builds a combo.
- Golden butterflies can start appearing after the player has already scored some points and are worth 5x the normal base value.
- The number of active targets increases as the score rises.
- The ordinary score display increments silently; special popups are reserved for golden targets.

## Target set

The target pool includes flowers, butterflies, birds, giraffes, monkeys, frogs, toucans, parrots, and hippos.

## Feedback and presentation

- The safari lake scene includes mountains, baobab trees, reeds, lily pads, dragonflies, puddles, ripples, mist, and sunbeams.
- Wet targets shake and glow as they near completion.
- Soaked targets explode into splash particles, and larger combos trigger extra fountain-style bursts.
- The elephant itself celebrates with bounces, happy cheeks, trunk trumpet arcs, and stronger ear animation.
