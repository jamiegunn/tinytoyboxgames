# Puppy Fetch

## What it is

Puppy Fetch is a one-tap fetch loop. The player throws a ball, watches the puppy sprint after it, and scores when the puppy brings it back.

## How it plays

1. In the waiting state, the puppy idles at center field with the ball.
2. Tapping anywhere throws the ball toward that spot.
3. The puppy automatically runs to the landed ball, grabs it, and returns home.
4. When the puppy gets back, the game awards points and plays a short celebration before allowing the next throw.

## Controls

- Tap anywhere on the field to throw the ball.

## Scoring and progression

- Each completed fetch scores at least 1 point.
- Completing fetches within 6 seconds of each other builds a combo.
- Combo points scale directly with the combo count.
- There is no miss state; the player simply waits for the current fetch cycle to finish.

## Feedback and presentation

- The scene includes hills, fence lines, trees, flowers, butterflies, birds, drifting leaves, paw prints, and dust clouds.
- Throwing the ball plays a whistle-like throw sound.
- Returning the ball plays a happy fetch sound, confetti, and a puppy celebration.
- Large combos can make the puppy jump, spin, and bark.
