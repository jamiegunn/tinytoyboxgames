# Balloon Race

## What it is

Balloon Race is a swipe-launch game. The player grabs balloons from a festival field and pulls them upward to send them flying.

## How it plays

1. A row of 3 to 5 balloons appears near the ground.
2. The player taps a balloon to grab it.
3. Dragging upward stretches the balloon.
4. Pulling far enough launches it into the sky; letting go too early makes it snap back.

## Controls

- Tap a balloon to grab it.
- Drag upward to launch it.
- Release before the threshold to cancel the launch.

## Scoring and progression

- Standard balloons are worth 1 point before combo bonuses.
- Golden balloons can appear after the player has launched several balloons and are worth 5 points before combo bonuses.
- Launching again within 3 seconds builds a combo.
- Combo points multiply the base balloon value.
- Once the current row is empty, a fresh row spawns automatically.

## Feedback and presentation

- The background is a bright fairground-style landscape with hills, bunting, birds, flowers, and clouds.
- Long combo streaks can trigger a rainbow in the sky.
- Launching uses a rising whoosh sound plus confetti.
- Each balloon has a face, pattern, and slightly different shape so the row feels playful instead of cloned.
