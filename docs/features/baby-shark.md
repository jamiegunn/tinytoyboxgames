# Baby Shark

## What it is

Baby Shark is an underwater chase game. The player steers a smiling shark around a reef and taps fish to make the shark hunt them down.

## How it plays

1. Several fish swim freely around the water.
2. Tapping a fish marks it as the chase target.
3. The shark lunges toward that fish and eats it on contact.
4. Dragging or tapping empty water instead makes the shark free-swim toward that point.

## Controls

- Tap a fish to command a direct chase.
- Tap or drag elsewhere to steer the shark manually.

## Scoring and progression

- Eating a fish scores at least 1 point.
- Eating again within 2.5 seconds builds a combo.
- Higher combo streaks grant more points and can trigger a short slow-motion effect.
- Special larger fish can appear later in the session, but the game still stays endless and forgiving.
- The game keeps repopulating fish so the scene never empties out.

## Feedback and presentation

- The environment includes coral, seaweed, bubbles, jellyfish, treasures, god rays, and drifting plankton.
- Nearby fish try to flee from the shark, which makes the chase feel lively without making it punishing.
- A quick bite sound and confetti play on every successful catch.
- Combo streaks show a "CHOMP xN" meter.
