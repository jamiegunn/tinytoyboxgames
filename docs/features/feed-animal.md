# Feed Animal

## What it is

Feed Animal is a tap-and-deliver game set in a cheerful meadow. The player taps floating food items and watches them arc into a waiting animal's mouth.

## How it plays

1. Several foods appear along the top area of the screen.
2. Tapping a food launches it toward the animal in the center-bottom picnic area.
3. When food reaches the animal, the animal reacts with chewing, bouncing, eye sparkles, hearts, crumbs, and score feedback.
4. Once all foods are used, a new batch spawns automatically.

## Controls

- Tap a food item to send it to the animal.

## Scoring and progression

- Every successful feed scores at least 1 point.
- Feeding again within 3 seconds grows a combo and raises the points awarded.
- A big celebration happens every 5 successful feeds.
- The featured animal rotates every 10 successful feeds through bear, panda, bunny, cat, and frog.
- Higher scores slightly increase how many food items appear in a batch.

## Feedback and presentation

- The game uses a garden scene with clouds, butterflies, flowers, and animated grass.
- Food launches leave little sparkles, and successful feeds throw off hearts and crumbs.
- Audio feedback is a two-part chomping sound.
- The combo meter appears when the player strings together quick feeds.
