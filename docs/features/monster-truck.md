# Monster Truck

## What it is

Monster Truck is an auto-running stunt game set on a scrolling dirt track in front of a stadium crowd.

## How it plays

1. The truck drives forward automatically over rolling terrain.
2. Tapping while grounded makes it jump.
3. Tapping again in the air spends the extra boost jump and adds more lift and spin.
4. The player crushes obstacles, collects floating stars, and can earn bonus points for flips on landing.

## Controls

- Tap once to jump.
- Tap again in the air to boost if an air jump is still available.

## Scoring and progression

- Crushing an obstacle awards points and builds the combo chain.
- Higher combo levels make each crush worth more.
- Collecting a floating star adds 2 points.
- Completing full flips before landing awards additional bonus points.
- Track speed rises gradually with score, so later runs feel faster and busier.

## Obstacle set

The crushable pool includes cars, buses, vans, tire stacks, boxes, porta-potties, and ramps.

## Feedback and presentation

- The scene uses a sunset sky, spotlights, a waving crowd, dirt tracks, flags, dust, exhaust, flame boosts, mud splatters, and speed lines.
- Jumps use an engine-rev sound; crushes use a heavy impact sound.
- Air boosts leave fire trails.
- Combo streaks escalate from regular combo text to "MEGA" feedback, while flip bonuses trigger bigger celebrations.
